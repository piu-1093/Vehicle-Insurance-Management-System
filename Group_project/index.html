CLAIMS PROCESS

Fast, Simple & Transparent Claims

We've simplified the entire claim journey so you can focus on getting back on the road while we take care of the paperwork and verification.

STEP 01

Report The Incident

Inform us immediately after an accident, theft or any vehicle damage using the online insurance portal.

STEP 02

Upload Documents

Submit all required documents including RC, Driving License, photographs and supporting evidence.

STEP 03

Verification

Our administrators and underwriters carefully verify your policy and claim details for faster approval.

STEP 04

Claim Approval

Once all documents are verified, your insurance claim is approved and processed securely.

STEP 05

Settlement

Receive your approved claim amount quickly through our secure and transparent settlement process.
